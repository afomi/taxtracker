## Overview

TaxTracker provides a transparent, data-rich view of taxation flows in the U.S.:

- **Inventory of Taxes**
  Explore 80+ federal, state, and local tax types, including income, property, sales, excise, and customs duties, plus user/license fees :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

- **Follow the Money**
  Prototype tool visualizing how tax dollars move from collection to eventual spending (e.g., *Sales Tax ‚Üí General Fund ‚Üí Parks Dept ‚Üí Vendor #8312*), with FY24 figures, e.g., $2.8M flows illustrated :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

- **Data Capacity & Licensing**
  Data updated **monthly**, covering state and local sources, and available under **CC BY 4.0** :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

- **APIs & Exports** *(Coming Soon)*
  Plans to enable CSV/JSON exports and API access‚Äîwith support funding options via Bitcoin SV donations :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.
