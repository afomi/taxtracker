Taxpayer associations

A directory of taxpayer associations — civic organizations that research and advocate on tax and public-spending issues. These are potential collaborators and data sources, not neutral publishers: most advocate for lower taxes or spending restraint. We list them for transparency and label them as advocacy organizations.
